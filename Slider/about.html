The Prefabricated Hedgehouse project sets out to address two essentially important issues
1. Increase the fertile areas of the planet by maximising vegetation growth in urban areas, and in particular enable plants to grow over buildings. This would partly compensate for those fertile areas that are being lost due to climate change.
2. The UK is now suffering a shortfall of at least 4M homes at a time when there has been a massive hike in the cost of labour and materials.
People have lived within the landform of the Earth for centuries but the horror of what was happening to the planet led to great interest in Earth Sheltering and how it can benefit accommodation.
Earth Sheltering recognised the importance of retaining soil and looking after nature . Now because of climate change and population growth, the need to care for the planet is more essential than ever
Vegetation plays a crucial part in protecting the Earth and preserving its balance so calamities are Minimised-this is what happens when that balance is lost
Alongside the crisis of the planet the UK is experiencing a housing crisis-a shortfall of at least 4 M homes. The problem is made worse because of the massive hike in the cost of labour and materials-so more efficient methods of building will have to be used-like Prefabrication
